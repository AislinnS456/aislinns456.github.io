Hey! I'm Aislinn ("Ace-lyn"), a 2nd year graduate student in the Mathematics Department at the University of Texas at Austin.

I enjoy learning about the interactions between topology, combinatorics, and group-theory. My current research focuses on the combinatorial and algorithmic complexity of surface braid groups, specifically questions related to the word problem, automata theory, and Garside structures. More broadly, I am interested in studying the topology of configuration spaces and braid-like structures, together with the operadic and homotopical ideas that connect them to geometry, knot theory, and representation theory.
