Hey! I'm Aislinn ("Ace-lyn"), a 3rd year graduate student in the Mathematics Department at the University of Texas at Austin.

I enjoy learning about the interactions between topology, geometry, and group theory.

My current research concerns the computational and combinatorial complexity of positive-genus surface braid groups, with emphasis on the word problem, automata theory, and Garside structures.

More broadly, I am interested in groups arising from the topology of configuration spaces and related structures, together with the homotopy-theoretic and operadic methods that relate these objects to low-dimensional topology, knot theory, and geometric group theory.
